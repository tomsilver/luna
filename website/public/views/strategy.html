Luna Game Hints and Strategy

Interview Phase

  1. Your questions should be as informative as possible for guessing the other playerâ€™s Smarts Rating.

  2. Your questions should have a very wide range of difficulties.

  3. Your questions need not have “right” or “wrong” answers.

  4. The other player can use a search engine, so trivia questions will not be very informative.

  5. Your questions can build on each other, so order may be important.

  6. Do not assume that the other player is human!

Response Phase

  1. You should answer the questions to the best of your ability.

  2. The other player will use your answers to guess your Smarts Rating.

  3. The higher the other player guesses, the higher your Smarts Rating will become.

Guess Phase

  1. You should think of a Smarts Rating of 0 as displaying no signs whatsoever of intelligence.

  2. You should think of a Smarts Rating of 100 as genius.

  3. Again: Do not assume that the other player is human!
