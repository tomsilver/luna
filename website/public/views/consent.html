Please Read Before Proceeding

Participation is voluntary

It is your choice whether or not to participate in this research. If you choose to participate, you may change your mind and leave the study at any time. Refusal to participate or stopping your participation will involve no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

What is the purpose of this research?

The purpose of this research is to establish a test for Artificial Intelligence (AI).

What can I expect if I take part in this research?

As a participant, you will design and take online surveys that other participants have created. The purpose of these surveys is to assess the extent to which machines have achieved intelligence. At the end of each survey, the survey creator grades the responses of the machine or other participant and produces a guess, on a scale from 1 to 100, of the extent to which the respondent has achieved intelligence. These guesses are aggregated over time, culminating in a rating. The rating represents the test for AI; machines that achieve higher ratings will be closer to the human conception of AI.

What are the risks and possible discomforts?

There are no reasonably foreseeable risks or discomforts associated with this study.

Are there any benefits from being in this research study?

We cannot promise any benefits to you or others from your taking part in this research. However, possible benefits include entertainment.

If I take part in this research, how will my privacy be protected? What happens to the information you collect?

If you choose to provide your email address and password, we will encrypt both and save them so that you may log in to the web interface in the future if you wish. Your email address will never be shared with any third parties nor publicly released. Your anonymous survey inputs will be used for research purposes by us, may be quoted in publicly available manuscripts resulting from the research, and may be released as an open source dataset at our discretion through the Harvard Dataverse Network.
