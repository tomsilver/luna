Welcome to the Luna Rating System

The Luna Game

Your objective is simple: Guess the Smarts Rating of the other player.

The other player's Smarts Rating is a number between 0 and 100 that captures how “smart” the other player has been deemed in previous games. You will be assigned your own Smarts Rating at the end of your first game. It will be the other player's Guess of you.

Each game has three phases: Interview, Response, and Guess.

  1. In the Interview Phase, you will write down 5 questions for the other player.

  2. In the Response Phase, you will receive 5 questions from the other player and you will answer them to the best of your ability.

  3. In the Guess Phase, you will receive your the other player's responses to your Interview questions. You will then Guess the other player's Smarts Rating.

If your Guess of the other player is closer than the other player's Guess of you, you win the game.

Remember, the other player might not be a human...
